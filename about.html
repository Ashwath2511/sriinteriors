Our History

The Standard Of Luxury Living

Sri Interiors began with a singular focus: to elevate standard residential spaces into bespoke sanctuaries. Over the past twelve years, we have worked with elite clients to craft premium layouts that balance function, elegance, and durability.

We control the design journey end-to-end. Our clients experience an absolute lack of stress, as our layout designers, wood craftsmen, and material curators synchronize to shape environments that feel like liveable art pieces.

Elite Sourcing

We partner with premium global suppliers to procure genuine marble, quartz, and Italian hinges.

Precision Mockups

Detailed 3D models ensure that what you approve matches the delivered reality precisely.

Our Principles

Built On Absolute Detail

Four pillars define every spatial layout and interior render that carries the Sri Interiors mark.

Bespoke Artistry

We reject mass template layouts. Every home design has custom wood grain selections and lighting configurations.

Uncompromising Trust

Transparency in material thickness, wood specifications, and project timeline execution is our code of honor.

Functional Balance

Luxury is useless if it is hard to clean. We blend automated technology with accessible storage layouts.

Eco Durability

Non-toxic paints, solid ply foundations, and energy-conserving layouts keep your home healthy.

How We Work

The Execution Journey

A carefully timed step-by-step roadmap from initial conceptualization to handing you the keys.

1

Initial Consultation

We discuss your style preferences, layout demands, functionality requirements, and project budget parameters.

2

Layout & Space Planning

Our design curators chart 2D flow plans, analyzing ergonomics, natural light positions, and structural walls.

3

3D Renders & Material Selection

You experience virtual tours of your space, selecting laminates, stones, and lighting models in our gallery catalog.

4

Artisan Execution

Our master carpenters, electricians, and painters shape the space using precise dimensions and clean mechanics.

5

Polishing & Final Delivery

We clean, adjust alignments, test lighting channels, and hand you a gorgeous, turn-key luxury home.
